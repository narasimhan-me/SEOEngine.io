# Jira Epic: EA-41 — ISSUE-TO-ACTION-GUIDANCE-1

**Type:** Epic  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Labels:** guidance, phase-e, issue-action, trust

---

## Summary

Guide users from issue → recommended action, with explanation.

---

## Problem

Users don't always understand which action makes sense for which issue.

---

## User Risk

Over-fixing, under-fixing, or hesitation due to uncertainty.

---

## Intent

Guide users from issue → recommended action, with explanation.

---

## Scope (IN)

- Issue-linked guidance panels
- Plain-English "why this action helps"
- Clear distinction between guidance vs execution

---

## Explicitly OUT of scope (HARD LINE)

- Auto-selection
- Auto-run
- Forced recommendations

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Users understand why an action is suggested
- Guidance is optional and dismissible
- No implied authority

---

## Notes

Phase E. Guidance only; no auto-selection or execution.
